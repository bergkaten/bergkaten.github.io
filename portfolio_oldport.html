The Spooktacular Lake Michigan Triangle

Project Details

  • Project date: Spring 2020
  • Tools: Esri ArcGIS Pro
  • Data: Styles, Textures, and Bathy by/from John Nelson | Triangle Location | Michigan Shipwrecks | NOAA Wrecks

Have you heard of the Bermuda Triangle of the Great Lakes? Numerous things have gone missing in this area, including many ships, a captain, and a plane. There has also been several accounts of strange lights and other happenings. Here's my interpretation of the mysterious zone.

"Here be dragons" is a remenant of old maps where catographers would put the phrase in unknown areas with potential danger.

There is also a nod to the mysterious Stonehenge found underwater in the scribbles at the bottom.
